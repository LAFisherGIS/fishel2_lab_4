Time and Place - A Geolocation Demonstration

If you press the blank button on the left, this map will request permission to geolocate the device you are accessing it from. This information will be used to zoom in on that estimated location, to provide an estimate of the level of accuracy regarding that estimate (a red circle), and to determine whether or not the sun is currently in the sky there to determine if a bright or dark map is better (if you disagree with it, you can use the control in the top right to manually reselect the color of the map). Please press the button only once, unless you move a substantial distance between clicks. This information will not be stored nor delivered to any outside party.
